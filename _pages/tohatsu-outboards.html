Tohatsu, Japan's oldest outboard manufacturer, has been producing high quality, durable outboard motors since 1956. Tohatsu outboards were originally developed for commercial Japanese fishermen who needed a motor that could run for days on end, no matter what the weather. Tohatsu offers a five-year warranty which shows their commitment to providing not only a great value, but a dependable motor with unmatched performance.
